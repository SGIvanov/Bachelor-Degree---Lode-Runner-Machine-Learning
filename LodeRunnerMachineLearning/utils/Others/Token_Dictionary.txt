#     | Normal Brick (can be break)
@     | Solid Brick (cannot be break)
H     | Ladder 
-     | Hand-to-hand bar (Line of rope)
X     | False brick (Player passes trough the brick unless is the end of the level)
S     | Ladder appears at end of level for the player to arrive at the new level
$     | Gold chest (Need to get all chests to end the level)
0     | Guard (Enemy)
&     | Player	(You)
